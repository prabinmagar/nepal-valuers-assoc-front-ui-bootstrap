Nepal Valuer’s Association (NVA) is the umbrella organisation of professional valuers of Nepal. It was established on Feb 6, 2012 under the “Organisation Registration Act 2034 B.S.” of Nepal. It is the first voluntary valuer’s organisation in the country founded by a group of valuers for the purpose of strengthening and promoting the valuation profession in Nepal. It also plays the role of a facilitator to address all issues related to valuer’s profession in the country. Its Headquarters is based in the capital,
